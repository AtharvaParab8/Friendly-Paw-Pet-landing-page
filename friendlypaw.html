FAQ

Do I need to book an appointment for walk-in services?

Walk-in services are unavailable for online or over the phone booking. Simply visit a FreindlyPaw Grooming Salon near you for these services. Subject to groomer's schedule.

What different grooming services does PawFriendly offer?

Your puppy can enjoy all our grooming services! We offer a puppy shampoo specifically baseds on your puppy's skin and coat, and all our shampoos are safe for puppies 12 weeks and older. We use high-quality, natural grooming products emphasizes the well-being of the pet.

How much training do Friendly Paw groomers receive?

The duration of on-the-job training can vary, but it may span to several months. The no of hours are about 400 hours. Certifications are given based on combination of training hours, practical experience, and passing an exam.

How long does a dog grooming service take?

The amount of time depends on your pet's size and the type of service requested. Groomer will be able to provide an estimated time at the time of appointment,appointment is necessary. However, we recommend calling your local salon to confirm availability. Generally,a full grooming session which includes haircut, bath, nail trimming, and other services takes about 2.5 hours.Dogs with mats or heavy shedding may require additional time for de-matting or de-shedding procedures, extending the grooming session.
